Security & Safety Model

Overwatch accelerates operational decision-making while preserving safety and human approval.

  • .env protected
  • No secrets committed
  • Read-only patch generation
  • No autonomous source mutation
  • Human approval required
  • Localhost-only dashboard
  • Deterministic replay support
